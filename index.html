Mission

What we're building and why

Standard mobile robot deployments require a pre-built map before autonomous navigation is possible. This creates a chicken-and-egg problem in unknown or dynamically changed environments.

Our system solves this by combining frontier-based exploration with semantic awareness — the robot builds, classifies, and navigates its environment simultaneously from scratch, with no prior map and no human guidance.

The robot semantically classifies regions as Clear, Cluttered, or Hazardous using its OAK-D RGB-D camera, producing a labeled occupancy map as output.
